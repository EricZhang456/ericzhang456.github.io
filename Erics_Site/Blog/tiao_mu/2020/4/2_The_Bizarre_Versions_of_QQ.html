Hi there, Eric here. In this blog post I am going to talk about the versions of QQ that released back in the day. The versions below won’t login since they are too old.


QQ Concept Edition


The QQ Concept Edition is probably the hype train of QQ back then. It even caught Chairman Hu’s attention. QQ is completely rewritten in WPF. Took full advantage of touch screen computing and WPF. This version added many features such as Desktop Buddy, Avatar Frame and much more. They even made a trailer back then.

















The QQ Concept Edition is available for download. However, the system requirement for QQ Concept Edition is too high, at least Windows Vista Service Pack 2 and score 3.5 in Windows Experience Index. QQ Concept Edition is not mouse friendly either, since it takes full advantage of touch screen. Most computers (in China) back then still has Windows XP installed. The QQ Concept Edition was quickly abandoned and ended at Beta 1.3.
